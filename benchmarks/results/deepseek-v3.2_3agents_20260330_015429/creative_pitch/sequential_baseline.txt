Introducing **LogCraft**, the developer tool that transforms your git commit history into polished, publication-ready changelog entries in seconds. Stop wasting hours manually sifting through commits before every release. LogCraft intelligently parses your commit messages, PR descriptions, and JIRA links using semantic analysis to automatically categorize changes into "Added," "Fixed," and "Breaking" sections. It enforces consistency, filters out noise like merges and chores, and generates clear, user-facing summaries that your product and support teams will love. Seamlessly integrate it into your CI/CD pipeline via a GitHub Action or CLI, and ship with confidence, knowing your release notes are always accurate, comprehensive, and never an afterthought. Reclaim your Fridays—let LogCraft handle the narrative.
