Tired of the tedious, error-prone chore of writing changelogs at the end of a sprint? **LogCraft** is the AI-powered developer tool that transforms your git commit history into professional, ready-to-ship changelog entries in seconds. Simply connect your repo, and LogCraft intelligently analyzes conventional commits, PR descriptions, and issue references to automatically categorize changes (think: `✨ Features`, `🐛 Fixes`, `🔧 Maintenance`), writes clear, human-readable summaries—not just commit hash dumps—and formats them to your specified style. It integrates directly into your CI/CD pipeline or GitHub Actions, so generating accurate, consistent release notes becomes a seamless, automated step, not a last-minute scramble. Stop letting documentation be a bottleneck; with LogCraft, you ship faster and keep everyone—from your team to your users—perfectly in sync with what actually changed.
