**Stop wasting hours manually curating changelogs. Ship Notes is the developer tool that instantly transforms your team’s git commit history into polished, ready-to-publish release notes.** By parsing conventional commits and linking directly to pull requests, it automatically categorizes changes into "Added," "Fixed," and "Breaking" sections, weaving together commit messages into coherent, user-friendly summaries. It integrates seamlessly into your CI/CD pipeline, ensuring every release—whether a major launch or a hotfix—gets an accurate, professional changelog entry without a single manual draft. Reclaim your Friday afternoons, eliminate human error, and ensure your users and stakeholders always have crystal-clear visibility into what’s new, all while enforcing better commit discipline across your team.
