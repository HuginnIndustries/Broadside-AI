Tired of manual changelog entries that get forgotten, inconsistent, or buried in release notes? Meet **ChangelogBot** — the AI-powered developer tool that scans your Git commit history, intelligently parses conventional commits (feat:, fix:, BREAKING CHANGE:, etc.), and auto-generates clean, human-readable, versioned changelogs in Markdown or JSON format — ready to paste into GitHub Releases, npm, or your internal wiki. No more guessing what changed last sprint, no more formatting headaches. Just commit, push, and let ChangelogBot turn your development rhythm into polished, auditable release documentation — so your team spends less time writing notes and more time shipping code that matters.
