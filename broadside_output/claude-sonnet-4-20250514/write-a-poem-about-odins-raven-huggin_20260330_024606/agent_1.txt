**Huginn's Flight**

Black wings beat through morning mist,
Odin's faithful thought takes flight,
Across the Nine Worlds, never missed,
From dawn's first gleam to fall of night.

Huginn soars on northern wind,
Through Asgard's halls and Midgard's plains,
What mortal eyes have never seen,
The raven's keen sight ascertains.

From battlefield to royal court,
From peasant's hut to mountain peak,
He gathers tales both long and short,
The secrets that all beings speak.

His feathers gleam like midnight oil,
His eyes reflect eternal flame,
Through ice and storm, through peace and toil,
He serves the All-Father's ancient name.

When shadows lengthen, home he flies,
To Odin's shoulder, his rightful place,
To whisper truths and worldly lies,
The gathered wisdom of each race.

O Huginn, raven dark and wise,
Thought's messenger on tireless wing,
Through all the realms beneath the skies,
You are the song that ravens sing.

With Muninn by your faithful side,
You are the eyes that never sleep,
In you, the gods' great knowledge hide—
The memories that Asgard keeps.
